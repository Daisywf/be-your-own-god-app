Privacy & Disclaimer

Last updated: June 2026

Be Your Own God is not therapy, medical care, or a substitute for professional help.

It is a space for reflection and self-companionship — a way to hear your own inner voice more clearly. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide clinical advice. If you are struggling, please also reach out to a qualified professional or someone you trust. You deserve real human support.

If you are in crisis

If you are thinking about harming yourself or ending your life, please reach out right now to people who can help:

  • International: Find a local helpline at findahelpline.com
  • United States: Call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • United Kingdom: Call 116 123 (Samaritans)
  • China (中国): 北京心理危机研究与干预中心 010-82951332 ／ 希望24热线 400-161-9995
  • Japan (日本): よりそいホットライン 0120-279-338

You are not alone. This moment is not the whole story.

Who we are

Be Your Own God (aizhibei.cc) is an independent project created to give anyone, anywhere, a warm and listening presence — modeled on the idea that the wisest, most loving voice you can hear is your own. We take the trust you place in us seriously, because the things you share here are often the things you've told no one else.

What we collect

  • Your account: email address and an encrypted (hashed) password. We never store your password in readable form.
  • Your conversations: if you create an account, your messages and the responses are stored so your conversation can continue across time and devices. If you use the site without an account, your conversation is kept only temporarily and is not permanently saved.
  • Payment information: handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • Basic technical data: minimal information needed to keep the service running securely.

How we use it

  • To let you have a continuous, remembered conversation with your inner voice.
  • To keep you signed in and to process donations or subscriptions.
  • To keep the service safe and working.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your private conversations to train AI models. We do not share them with advertisers. Your words are yours.

How your conversations are processed

To generate responses, your messages are sent securely to our AI provider (Anthropic) solely to produce a reply to you. They are not used by us for advertising or profiling.

Your choices

  • Delete your data: you can request deletion of your account and all stored conversations at any time by contacting us at the email below. We will honor it.
  • Export: you can request a copy of your stored conversations.
  • Cancel anytime: subscriptions can be canceled at any time and will not renew afterward.

Data security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we treat your conversations as deeply private and design accordingly.

Children

This service is intended for adults (18+). It is not designed for children. If you are a young person in distress, please talk to a trusted adult or a helpline listed above.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with a revised date.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else: support@aizhibei.cc
